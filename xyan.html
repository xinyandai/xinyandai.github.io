Biography:

I am a Ph.D candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of James Cheng. Before coming to CUHK, I got my bachelor degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China and my master degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

Research Interest:

My interests lie at the intersection of database and machine learning, including probabilistic algorithms, similarity search, machine learning, and systems for large-scale machine learning
