Xinyan Dai

I am a Ph.D candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of James Cheng. Before coming to CUHK, I got my bachelor degree from Nanjing University in 2018.

My research mainly focuses on Similarity Search and Machine Learning. Typically, I am interested in applying similarity search techniques (Locality Sensitive Hashing, Sketching, Vector Quantization) on large scale machine learning.
